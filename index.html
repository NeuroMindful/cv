I am an Associate Professor of Neuroscience at Xavier University of Louisiana. My research emphasizes structural and functional brain networks associated emotional processing, attention, mindfulness behavior and systems underlying health benefits of meditation. My work has been supported by NIH and the LA Board of Regents. I am a member of Cognitive Neuroscience Society, Organization of Human Brain Mapping, Society for Neuroscience and affiliated with an international consortium of researchers invested in Real-time Functional Imaging and Neurofeedback.
